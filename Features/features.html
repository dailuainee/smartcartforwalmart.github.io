Tablet Screen Demonstration

Click the slider buttons on the slideshow below to navigate through the SmartCart tablet screen.

Screen Image Descriptions

  1. Welcome Screen = The first screen displayed on the SmartCart, greeting the customer with a welcome message.
  2. Home Page = Default page that displays different items (as well as their designated locations) relative to items previously scanned. Can also suggest featured items on sale.
  3. Interactive Map = Shows a designated map displaying the layout of the Walmart store. Uses built-in tracker to show where the customer (the SmartCart device) is within the store. Can point to the location of a item the customer desires if they click the "Show on Map" button shown on the previous page.
  4. Your Cart Screen = Displays all items within customer's cart. Customer has an option to delete any items. Features a "Check Out" button if the customer wishes to check out their items.
  5. Receipt Screen = Displayed after user checks out with a payment method. Directs customer to confirm receipt with Walmart employee upon their exit.
